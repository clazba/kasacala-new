The Play Pillar

The Art of Leisure

A curated sanctuary where sun-drenched afternoons dissolve into vibrant evenings. At Casa Kala, play is not an afterthought—it is a vital frequency for the modern soul.

Recharge. Enjoy. Recalibrate.

Designed for our members to navigate the lighter side of the club. Whether it's a silent morning swim or a competitive night in the games lounge, the Play Pillar is your dedicated space to shed the weight of the work week.
